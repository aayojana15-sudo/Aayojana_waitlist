Section 01

How Our Escrow System Works

Aayojana's advance protection is built around a simple principle: your money stays with us until your event is delivered. We do not release any advance payment to a vendor before the event takes place.

1
Booking Confirmed
Customer pays the advance on-platform. Aayojana holds it in escrow. Vendor is notified.
2
Event Day
Vendor delivers services. Customer attends the event.
3
48-Hour Dispute Window
Customer has 48 hours after the event date to raise a dispute on the Platform. If no dispute is raised, the advance is automatically released to the vendor.
✓
Payout Released
Vendor receives the advance minus Aayojana's commission, within 3–5 business days.

The escrow hold is the foundation of Aayojana's advance protection. Customers who pay vendors directly outside the Platform are not covered by this protection.

Section 03

Vendor Cancellations

If a vendor cancels a confirmed booking for any reason, the customer is entitled to a full refund of the advance amount paid on-platform.

Vendor cancellations are the only scenario where refunds are guaranteed without any waiting period or dispute process. We process the refund immediately upon confirming the cancellation.

Additionally, the vendor is subject to a cancellation penalty of ₹2,000 per incident, escalating to suspension and delisting for repeat cancellations, as defined in the Vendor Agreement.

If a vendor fails to show up on the event day without prior notice, this is treated as a vendor cancellation and the customer is entitled to a full refund along with all applicable vendor penalties.

Section 05

Post-Event Disputes

If a customer is dissatisfied with the service received, they must raise a dispute through the Platform within 48 hours of the event date. Disputes raised after this window cannot be considered.

What constitutes a valid dispute

  • The vendor failed to show up or abandoned the event midway
  • The service delivered was materially different from the accepted booking description
  • The venue or setup was unsafe or unusable
  • A core part of the package (e.g. main décor, catering, photography) was not delivered

What is not a valid dispute

  • Minor aesthetic differences from reference photos or sample images
  • Personal preference or subjective dissatisfaction with delivered work
  • Issues caused by the customer (e.g. late arrivals, changes made on the day)
  • Weather or external factors outside the vendor's control

Dispute resolution process

  1. 01 Customer raises dispute with evidence (photos, video, written description) through the Platform
  2. 02 Aayojana reviews the evidence and contacts both parties within 3 business days
  3. 03 Both parties may submit supporting documentation
  4. 04 Aayojana issues a resolution decision within 7 business days of the dispute being raised
  5. 05 If upheld: full refund to customer. If not upheld: advance released to vendor.

Aayojana's dispute resolution decision is final. Disputes raised beyond 48 hours of the event date will not be accepted regardless of circumstances.

Section 06

Force Majeure

In the event of a force majeure occurrence — including natural disasters, government-imposed curfews, declared state emergencies, or pandemic-related restrictions that prevent the event from taking place — a full refund will be issued to the customer, provided that:

  • The force majeure event directly prevents the event from being held on the booked date
  • Supporting documentation (government order, news report, official notification) is submitted to Aayojana within 48 hours of the cancellation
  • The cancellation is reported through the Platform before the event date where possible

Force majeure refunds are processed within 7 business days of document verification and are not subject to the standard customer cancellation deductions.

Section 07

Refund Processing

Timelines

  • Vendor cancellation refunds — processed within 2 business days of confirmed cancellation
  • Customer cancellation refunds — processed within 5 business days of cancellation request
  • Dispute-upheld refunds — processed within 3 business days of Aayojana's resolution decision
  • Force majeure refunds — processed within 7 business days of document verification

Refund method

All refunds are credited back to the original payment source (UPI ID, bank account, or payment gateway used at the time of booking). Refunds cannot be redirected to a different account or payment method.

Razorpay processing charges (if any) deducted at the time of the original payment are non-refundable by Aayojana, as these are third-party charges outside our control.

Section 08

Payments Made Outside the Platform

Aayojana provides zero protection for payments made directly to vendors outside the Platform — whether via cash, personal UPI, bank transfer, or any other method. If a vendor solicits direct payment for a booking initiated through Aayojana, you should report it immediately to aayoajan15@gmail.com.

Customers who bypass the Platform's payment system cannot raise disputes, claim refunds, or seek Aayojana's intervention for any outcome related to that transaction.

Section 09

Platform Commission

Aayojana's platform commission is deducted only on completed and settled bookings, at the point of vendor payout. Commission is not charged on refunded transactions.

In the case of partial refunds (customer cancellations), the commission is calculated on the amount retained by the vendor, not on the full advance amount.
