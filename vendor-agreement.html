Section 01

Nature of Relationship

This Agreement governs the terms under which the Vendor lists and offers services on the Aayojana Platform. The Vendor is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent, partner, or joint venturer of Aayojana. Nothing in this Agreement creates any employment relationship.

Aayojana provides the technology infrastructure, payment processing, customer discovery, and trust mechanisms. The Vendor provides the actual event services. Aayojana is not responsible for the quality, delivery, or outcome of Vendor services.

Section 02

Vendor Eligibility and Onboarding

To be listed on Aayojana, the Vendor must:

  • Be a legally operating business or individual service provider in India
  • Submit valid KYC documents: Aadhaar card, PAN card, and GST registration (if applicable)
  • Provide accurate business name, service category, location, and contact details
  • Submit a valid bank account or UPI ID for payouts
  • Sign and accept this Agreement digitally during the onboarding process
  • Comply with all applicable local, state, and national laws governing the provision of event services

Aayojana reserves the right to approve or reject any Vendor application at its sole discretion.

Section 03

Listing Standards and Obligations

3.1 Accuracy

The Vendor must ensure that all listing content — including service descriptions, photographs, pricing, inclusions, exclusions, and availability — is accurate, complete, and not misleading at all times.

3.2 Pricing Transparency

All prices displayed must be all-inclusive for the described package.

Hidden charges, last-minute additions, or charges not disclosed at the time of listing are strictly prohibited and constitute a breach of this Agreement.

3.3 Availability

The Vendor must maintain an accurate availability calendar. Accepting bookings for dates that are already committed to other clients is a material breach of this Agreement.

3.4 Photographs

Listing photographs must accurately represent the Vendor's actual services. Use of photographs from other venues or decorators without disclosure is prohibited.

Section 04

Booking Process Obligations

4.1 Response Window

The Vendor must respond to all incoming booking requests (accept or decline) within 24 hours of receipt. Failure to respond within this window results in automatic expiry of the booking request.

4.2 Service Delivery

Upon accepting a booking and receiving the confirmed advance payment notification from Aayojana, the Vendor is contractually obligated to deliver the agreed services on the confirmed date, as described in the accepted booking.

4.3 Vendor Cancellations

Vendor-initiated cancellations are taken extremely seriously. In the event of a Vendor cancellation:

1st
Customer receives a full refund · Vendor receives no compensation · Cancellation penalty of ₹2,000 applied
2nd
Second cancellation within 6 months results in a 30-day suspension
3rd
Permanent delisting from the Platform

Force majeure cancellations (natural disasters, government-mandated closures) are exempt from penalties upon submission of supporting documentation within 48 hours.

Section 05

Commission Structure

5.1 Commission Rate

Aayojana charges a commission on each completed booking. The current rates are:

Vendor Tier Monthly Fee Commission Rate
Basic (Free) ₹0 8% of booking value
Growth ₹999/month 6% of booking value
Premium ₹2,499/month 4% of booking value

Launch period: 5% flat commission for all vendors onboarded during the first 6 months of platform operation, regardless of tier.

5.2 Commission Deduction

Commission is automatically deducted from the advance payment at the point of payout to the Vendor. No separate invoice or payment from the Vendor is required.

5.3 GST on Commission

GST at 18% is applicable on Aayojana's commission amount and is collected from the Vendor as per applicable tax law.

5.4 Commission Changes

Aayojana may revise commission rates with 30 days written notice to the Vendor. Continued use of the Platform after the notice period constitutes acceptance of the revised rates.

Section 06

Payment and Payout Terms

6.1 Advance Collection

All advance payments from Customers are collected by Aayojana through the Platform's payment gateway. Vendors must not request or accept advance payments directly from Platform-sourced customers.

6.2 Escrow Hold

Advance payments are held by Aayojana in escrow until one of the following:

  • The Customer confirms event completion through the Platform (triggers immediate payout processing)
  • 48 hours elapse after the event date with no dispute raised (triggers automatic payout)
  • A dispute is resolved in the Vendor's favour (triggers payout of disputed amount)

6.3 Payout Processing

Payouts are processed within 3–5 business days of the payout trigger. Payouts are made to the Vendor's registered bank account or UPI ID.

6.4 Payout Deductions

The following are deducted from payout:

Aayojana commission As per agreed tier
GST on commission 18% of commission
Cancellation penalties If applicable
Amounts owed from prior disputes If applicable
Section 07

Anti-Bypass Policy

⚠ Critical Clause

This clause is critical and its violation constitutes the most serious breach of this Agreement.

7.1 Prohibited Actions

The Vendor must not, under any circumstances:

  • Share personal phone numbers, email addresses, or social media handles with customers before booking confirmation
  • Encourage, suggest, or facilitate payment outside the Aayojana Platform for any booking initiated through the Platform
  • Offer discounts to customers in exchange for booking directly (bypassing the Platform)
  • Accept cash, UPI transfers, or any form of direct payment from Platform-sourced customers for Platform-initiated bookings

7.2 Detection Mechanisms

Aayojana employs the following mechanisms to detect bypass attempts:

Keyword monitoring in Platform messaging for phone numbers, external links, and bypass language
Customer reporting system for bypass attempts
Periodic customer verification calls post-event
Unusual booking pattern analysis

7.3 Consequences of Bypass

1st
Written warning + invoice for estimated commission on the bypassed transaction
2nd
90-day suspension + commission invoice
3rd
Permanent delisting + legal action for breach of contract
Section 08

Intellectual Property

By listing on Aayojana, the Vendor grants Aayojana a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use the Vendor's listing photographs, business name, and description for Platform marketing and promotional purposes.

The Vendor represents that they own or have the right to use all content submitted to the Platform and that such content does not infringe any third-party intellectual property rights.

Section 09

Confidentiality

The Vendor agrees to keep confidential all information about Aayojana's commission structure, technology, pricing algorithms, customer data, and business strategy that is shared during onboarding or operation. This obligation survives termination of this Agreement for a period of 2 years.

Section 10

Representations and Warranties

The Vendor represents and warrants that:

  • They have the legal right to provide the services listed on the Platform
  • Their business complies with all applicable local, state, and national laws
  • They hold all necessary licenses and permits for their services (FSSAI for caterers, etc.)
  • All information provided to Aayojana is accurate and complete
  • They will maintain the standards required for Aayojana's verified vendor badge
Section 11

Term and Termination

11.1 Term

This Agreement commences on the date of acceptance and continues until terminated by either party.

11.2 Termination by Vendor

The Vendor may terminate this Agreement by providing 30 days written notice. All confirmed bookings within the notice period must still be fulfilled.

11.3 Termination by Aayojana

Aayojana may terminate this Agreement immediately and without notice for:

  • Breach of the anti-bypass policy
  • Providing false KYC information
  • Repeated service delivery failures
  • Fraudulent activity
  • Any conduct that damages Aayojana's reputation or trust infrastructure

11.4 Effect of Termination

Upon termination, the Vendor's listing is removed from the Platform. All pending payouts for completed bookings will be processed normally. Commission on pending bookings remains payable.

Section 12

Dispute Resolution Between Vendor and Aayojana

Any dispute arising from this Agreement shall first be attempted through good-faith negotiation within 15 days. If unresolved, disputes shall be referred to arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, with a sole arbitrator appointed by mutual consent. The seat of arbitration shall be Bhubaneswar, Odisha. The language of arbitration shall be English.

Section 13

Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of India. The courts of Bhubaneswar, Odisha shall have exclusive jurisdiction over all disputes arising from this Agreement.

Section 14

Entire Agreement

This Agreement, together with the Terms of Service, Refund Policy, and Privacy Policy (all available at the Platform), constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior understandings.
