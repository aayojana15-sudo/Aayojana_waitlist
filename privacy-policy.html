Section 01

Introduction

Aayojana Technologies Private Limited ("Aayojana", "we", "our", "us") operates the platform aayojana-waitlist.vercel.app and its associated services (collectively, the "Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information when you access or use our Platform.

By accessing or using Aayojana, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Platform immediately.

Section 02

Information We Collect

2.1 — Information You Provide Directly
  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • City and location
  • Type of event you are planning
  • Business name (for vendors)
  • Service category (for vendors)
  • Bank account details (for vendor payouts — collected at onboarding)
  • KYC documents — Aadhaar, PAN, GST number (for vendors only)
2.2 — Information Collected Automatically
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referral source (how you found us)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
2.3 — Information from Third Parties
  • Payment information via Razorpay (we do not store card or UPI details directly)
  • Social login data if you connect via Google or similar OAuth provider
Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To process your waitlist registration and contact you at launch
  • To facilitate bookings between customers and vendors
  • To process and hold advance payments in escrow
  • To verify vendor identities through KYC
  • To release vendor payouts after event completion
  • To send booking confirmations, reminders, and receipts
  • To resolve disputes between customers and vendors
  • To improve the Platform through analytics and usage data
  • To send platform updates, feature announcements, and promotional communications (you may opt out at any time)
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulatory obligations
Section 04

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties under any circumstances.

We share your information only in the following situations:

4.1 With Vendors

Customer first name, event type, and event date are shared with vendors upon booking confirmation. Full contact details are never shared until a booking is confirmed on-platform.

4.2 With Service Providers

We share data with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Platform, including:

  • Razorpay — payment processing
  • Vercel — platform hosting
  • Google Analytics — usage analytics
  • SendGrid / Resend — email communications
  • MSG91 / Twilio — SMS and WhatsApp notifications

All service providers are contractually bound to use your data only for the purposes for which it was shared.

4.3 With Legal Authorities

We may disclose your information to law enforcement, courts, or regulatory authorities if required by applicable Indian law, including under the Information Technology Act, 2000 and its amendments.

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

Section 05

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored on secure servers hosted within or accessible from India
  • We implement industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for all data in transit
  • Sensitive vendor data (KYC documents, bank details) is stored with additional encryption at rest
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorised Aayojana personnel on a need-to-know basis
  • We conduct periodic security reviews of our infrastructure

Despite these measures, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security and are not liable for unauthorised access beyond our reasonable control.

Section 06

Data Retention

  • Waitlist signup data is retained until you request deletion or 24 months from collection, whichever is earlier
  • Active user account data is retained for the duration of your account plus 3 years after closure
  • Transaction records are retained for 7 years as required under Indian tax and financial regulations
  • KYC documents are retained for the period required by applicable law

To exercise any of these rights, email us at aayoajan15@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.
