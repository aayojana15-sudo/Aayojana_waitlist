5.2 Booking Process

  1. Customers initiate a booking request by selecting a vendor listing and submitting required event details
  2. The vendor has a defined response window to accept or decline
  3. Upon vendor acceptance, the Customer receives a payment link for the advance amount
  4. The booking is confirmed only after advance payment is received on-platform

5.3 Advance Payment

Advance amounts are held by Aayojana in escrow and are not released to vendors until event completion is confirmed.
